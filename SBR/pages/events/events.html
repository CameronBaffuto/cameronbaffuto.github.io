Planning on attending the Suncoast Basset Sweetheart Waddle?

PLEASE FOLLOW THESE SIMPLE RULES BELOW:

  • All dogs must be registered to attend. The first 150 dogs registered will receive a registration bag
  • All dogs must be current on their shots, which includes rabies
  • No puppies under the age of 4 months will be allowed
  • No female dogs in heat will be allowed
  • Any dogs showing aggression will be REQUIRED to leave immediately
  • All Bassets must have a bandanna to participate in the games. Sorry, the games are for the Bassets only
  • NO ALCOHOL and NO COOLERS are allowed at the Waddle. There will be food & drinks available for purchase. The concession stand will have a variety of foods for different dietary restrictions


$10 per K-9 per night fee applies

Guests should call the hotel directly to book and state they are with Suncoast Basset Rescue
