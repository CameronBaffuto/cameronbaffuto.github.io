Charlie is a sweet and loving 8 year old boy. He is very social and loves to give kisses. Charlie is extremely obedient and lives with 6 other Bassets but can be territorial of his owner and space. He would be best in a home as an only dog. He is fine with cats and actually likes to play with them. Charlie loves to ride in cars, go for walks, is housebroken, sits for treats and loves his human. If you can give Charlie his forever home, submit your application today.
