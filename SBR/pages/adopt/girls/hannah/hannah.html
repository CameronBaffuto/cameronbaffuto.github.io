Hannah


Previous Next

Hi, my name is Hannah. I am basset/wire-haired terrier mix, approximately 5-6 years-old. I am one of the “Miami 7” that Suncoast took in in June after being rescued from a hoarder.
Even though I have spent my entire life in squalor, I am a very well behaved girl. I have good manners and I mind for the most part. I am super sweet and love to cuddle on my own terms. I am finding it very enjoyable now that I know my foster mom is an okay human. Having not been socialized well, I do need to time to adjust in my new home. I am wary upon meeting new people but I do warm up. I will need a family that will keep this in mind.
I am going to need time and patience and lots and lots of love. I love playing with my foster mom and with my foster fur-siblings. I don’t mind cats either. I would do best in a home with dogs that are not smaller than me and no beagles. I do like the company of other playful dogs and also find comfort and security having a fur friend around, so my forever home would need to have other dogs. Luckily I didn’t suffer and lasting health issues, from my previous home, that the vet can find at this time.
My new home would also need a yard of my own to do zoomies in ~ oh how I love doing zoomies, and to be one with nature when I so choose. So how about it? Do I sound like the girl for you? If so, fill out an Adoption Application.
