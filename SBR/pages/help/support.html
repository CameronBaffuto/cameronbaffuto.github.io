Suncoast Basset Rescue is now part of iGive and you have the power to make your online purchases count! To participate, simply go to iGive and become a member. Once you join, you can select Suncoast as your favorite cause by searching for the Keywords "Suncoast" and "Basset". If you have any questions or would like additional information, please contact us here. Every adorable Basset at Suncoast Basset Rescue appreciates your generosity in joining iGive and selecting us as your cause of choice!

GoodSearch is a Yahoo!-powered search engine that you use exactly as you would any other search engine. The difference is each time you do a search, about a penny is raised for your favorite non-profit or school. The more you search, the more you earn for your cause and combined with millions of others in the Goodsearch movement, your searches can make a real impact. Go to Goodsearch, select "choose a cause" and enter "Suncoast Basset" to start making a difference in a Hounds life! Once you've signed up with Goodsearch, you can also support SBR through these amazing sites:
